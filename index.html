Briefing Prep 💡

Non-verbal Skills

Before you start, remember these key points for a professional briefing:

Eye Contact

Maintain steady eye contact with your audience to build trust.

Posture

Stand straight and confident. Avoid crossing your arms.

Facial Expression

Smile naturally to appear approachable and friendly.

Click PPT

Remember to click the presentation slides at the right moment to keep your audience engaged.
